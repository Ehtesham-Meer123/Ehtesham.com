Who Am I?

Azam Choudry is the principal of The Punjab School Johar Town Campus in Lahore. The campus, which became operational in 2007, spans 15 kanals and consists of two separate academic blocks: one for Junior & Girls High School and another for Boys High School. This setup caters to around 3300 students, offering a conducive academic atmosphere aimed at excellence​ (The Punjab School)​​ (Schoolvisor)​. The Punjab School is known for its commitment to providing high-quality education at an affordable cost. It was established with the goal of offering excellent education to children from lower-middle-class and indigent families. The school system is spread across multiple campuses in Lahore, each designed to meet the educational needs of a large number of students​ (The Punjab School)​​ (The Punjab School)​.
